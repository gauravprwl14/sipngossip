Welcome. Sip & Gossip is one of the most elegant restuarant in Bhilwara City and is the most favourite hangout place for soft drinks and conversation. This elegant restuarant was founded in April 2016 by Mr. Ashish Mundra and Mr. Rajat Pandey. Both are visionary leaders and successful entrepreneurs.

Sip & Gossip is a vertical of group company May I Help You whose directors are Mr. Vikas Babel and Mr. Dileep Jain.

The brand caters to the youth population through flavours of Indian food with different of varities like Chinese, Italian etc.
