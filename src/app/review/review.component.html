Of Our Guest

Ashish Mundra

First of all, the way they veganize all of the vegan stuff is pretty amazing. The constant innovation that they do to their dishes is something which is quite unique. I really like the concept of labelling of all of their food items, as this makes the consumer aware of what they're eating. They have range of desserts from raw vegan dishes like Ras Malai, Doodh Ke Ras Gulle to highly processed (:-P) and absolutely delicious stuff like Gulab Jamun, Ras Gulla etc.! You can also pickup their amazing starters off the shelf, which are priced very reasonably.

Dilip Mundra

The restaurant is a resounding proof that vegan food can be lip smackingly delicious and healthy. The widespread menu and sheer deliciousness establish that vegan lifestyle is no compromise, even for a foodie. Every time I visit Sip & Gossip, I am totally amazed by another dish. Cheese Corn Ball and Sunhari Tikki are winners. Ice cream platter is delicious, light and perfect for three. Lemon Ice Tea tastes delicious!! Doodh Ke Ras Gulle Dessert is just out of this world.

Sushant Mundra

I had lunch at Sip & Gossip and loved the food! The menu is huge and you can order from Indian specialties to variety of food - everything! I had a very delicious Curry! The Lemon Ice Tea was the best I ever had and the chocolate dessert was also like heaven! Besides the great food, the service is excellent. welcome drink are amazing and in the end desserts make your day perfect. They serve you water for free all the time and are very helpful & friendly. I spend hours there because I felt so comfortable. I definitely recommend to eat there!
